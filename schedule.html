Common Questions

Do I need my own piano to start?

Yes — for in-person lessons, you'll need a piano or keyboard at home since Lynn comes to you. A full-size (88-key) weighted keyboard is a great starting point and more affordable than an acoustic piano. Lynn is happy to give recommendations during your intro call.

What is the minimum age to start lessons?

Lynn welcomes students as young as 4 years old. At this age, lessons are short (30 minutes), playful, and focused on building a joyful relationship with the instrument. Every child develops differently, so the intro call is a great way to assess readiness.

How often are lessons?

Weekly lessons are strongly recommended for consistent progress. This gives students enough time to practice between sessions while maintaining good momentum. Lynn can discuss scheduling options during your introductory call.

Which lesson length should I choose?

The 30-minute lesson ($50) is recommended for children ages 4–8 who are just starting out. The 45-minute lesson ($70) suits most pre-teens and intermediate students. The 60-minute lesson ($80) is ideal for teens, adults, and more advanced students who want deeper musical exploration. Lynn will help you choose during the intro call.
