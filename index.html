Meet Your Teacher

Lynn Park Composer & Piano Educator

Lynn Park is a formally trained composer and pianist who graduated from Rutgers University with a BA in Music Composition. Her music is nostalgic, melancholy, and calming — marked by lyrical expressiveness and a seamless blend of classical structure with contemporary sensibility.

Lynn established her piano teaching practice in 2025 with a clear purpose: to help students genuinely understand and love music. Her compositional background shapes every lesson, giving students insight into not just how to play — but why music moves us.
