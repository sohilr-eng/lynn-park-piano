What to Expect

Lessons for Every Level

Whether you're picking up the piano for the very first time or refining an advanced repertoire, Lynn's curriculum is designed to meet you where you are — and help you truly love what you're learning.

🌱

Beginner

Perfect for students with little or no prior piano experience. We start with the fundamentals in a way that's engaging and encourages genuine curiosity about music from day one.

  • Basic technique & proper hand position
  • Note reading on the grand staff
  • Simple scales, chords & arpeggios
  • Introduction to music theory
  • Carefully selected beginner repertoire
🎹

Intermediate

For students with a foundational grasp of the piano who are ready to develop greater technical fluency, musical expression, and a deeper understanding of the music they play.

  • Expanded technique & finger independence
  • Repertoire from Classical, Romantic & modern eras
  • Harmony, voice-leading & basic composition
  • Sight-reading development
  • Understanding the "why" behind the music
🎼

Advanced

For committed pianists working at a high level who wish to deepen their artistry, tackle challenging repertoire, and develop a sophisticated musical voice through compositional understanding.

  • Advanced concert repertoire & interpretation
  • Compositional analysis & style study
  • Original composition & improvisation
  • Performance preparation & stage presence
  • Audition & conservatory prep (on request)
How We Meet

Lesson Formats

Choose the format that fits your lifestyle — or combine both as needed.

🏠

In-Person Lessons at Your Home

Lynn comes to you. In-person lessons are held at the student's home in New Jersey, primarily serving the Princeton area — with willingness to expand to surrounding communities. All you need is a piano or keyboard at home.

  • ✓ Convenient — no travel for you or your child
  • ✓ Hands-on technical guidance
  • ✓ Princeton area & surrounding NJ communities
💻

Online Lessons via Zoom

Fully interactive online lessons conducted via Zoom — available to students anywhere. All you need is an internet connection, a piano or keyboard, and a device. A great option for students outside the Princeton area.

  • ✓ Learn from anywhere
  • ✓ No commute needed
  • ✓ Flexible scheduling
