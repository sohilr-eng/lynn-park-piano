Teaching Philosophy

Lynn established her piano studio in [YEAR] with a clear vision: to offer lessons that go far beyond scales and exam pieces. Her teaching is informed by her compositional background — she wants students to understand music, not merely reproduce it.

Every student learns differently, and Lynn takes time to understand each person's goals, learning style, and musical interests before tailoring a curriculum. For younger students, she prioritises playfulness and curiosity. For teens and adults, she introduces compositional concepts early, so students can hear the 'why' behind every technical exercise.
